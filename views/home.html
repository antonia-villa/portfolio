I’m a full stack developer who refuses to believe data visualization’s only role is as a scientific tool for data analytics. A visualization can break down numbers into patterns, trends, and make information interesting and even beautiful. My goal is to make data more accessible to the people and empower them to learn from it rather than shy away from its complexities. A piece of art can inspire, impress, and make you think, and so should a good data visualization.
