Hey there!

I’m a full stack developer who refuses to believe data visualization’s only role is as a scientific tool for data analytics. Before I took the dive into the engineering world, I worked as an analyst by day and an artist by night. I quickly discovered data representation needed a makeover. Visualization can break down numbers into patterns, trends, and make information interesting and even beautiful. My goal is to make data more accessible to the people and empower them to learn from it rather than shy away from its complexities. A piece of art can inspire, impress, and make you think, and so should a good data visualization.
